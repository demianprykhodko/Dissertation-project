Here is the overall navigation of the application illustrated as a graph.

You can choose between 2 section 'routing' and 'queueing'

In the routing section you will be able to find the chargers you need for your journey. Just enter information in all of the inputs and choose one of 3 algorithms.

The 'Get All' algorithm will return all the chargers close to your desired route

The 'Optimal Custom' will let you choose a charger each time it is needed base on your inluts

The 'Optimal' algorithm will return the best chargers without any input from you accroding just to the algorithm

In the queueing section you will be able to find the chargers that are located nearby and try to reserve it or charge your car. Just enter information in all of the inputs and choose 'get nearby chargers' to find the chargers close to you.
